What is otome?

Otome (乙女ゲーム, lit. "maiden game") is a Japanese genre of visual novels that is targeted towards women. Usually, they are centered around developing a romantic relationship between the female main character (MC) and the male leads (ML).

Here, you can learn all about the otome genre, its history, get recommendations, various otome-related media, and its sister and umbrella genre: joseimuke!
