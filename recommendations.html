Popular Otome Games

There are so many Otome games, it can be hard to figure out where to start! Luckily, looking at the most popular otome games can give you good recommendations. I also have my personal recommendations below, which has a small overlap with the popular games.

From left to right: AMNESIA, BUSTAFELLOWS, Hakuouki: Kyoto Winds, and Cupid Parasite.
Click on the images to be sent to their VNDB page!

AMNESIA: Memories is considered a modern classic. It is one of the most iconic otomes, and is many people's first otome. You play as a woman who wakes up in-between worlds, without her memories, due to a collision with a fairy named Orion. He becomes trapped within her consciousness, and in order to leave, she must regain her memories. However, she must keep her amnesia a secret from those around her.

BUSTAFELLOWS is a newer otome, but no less exciting. You take on the role of Teuta Bridges, a journalist with the ability to go in the past using the bodies of others. After witnessing the murder of an infamous laywer named Limbo, she goes back in time to try and save him.

Hakuouki: Kyoto Winds is another classic otome. It is a historical fiction set in Japan's Bakumatsu period--a time inbetween the Shogunate era and the Meiji era. You play as Chizuru Yukimura, who goes to Kyoto to find her missing father, but ends up with the Shinsengumi, a group of warriors who defends the shogunate even as it crumbles. Somehow, Chizuru suspects they are hiding something amongst them.

Cupid Parasite is the most recent release out of all of these. You play as Lynette Mirror, the top bridal advisor in Los York, and secretly the goddess of love, Cupid. She's been sent to Earth to matchmaking the infamous Parasite 5--five men who are hopeless in love. They create the Parasite House, the setting for a reality TV centered around them and their adventures. Unfortunately, these men are proving to be difficult to matchmake even for the goddess of love! Can she keep her real identity a secret and help these men find love?

My Personal Recommendations

Tokimeki Memorial Girl's Side 2nd Season

★★★★★

Genre: Slice of Life

Languages: Japanese, English


You play as a high school student at Hanegasaki High School. While attending classes, clubs, and part-time jobs, you can grow your relationship with the male students that might lead to a declaration of love on your graduation day.

Mystic Messenger

★★★★☆

Genre: Mystery, Drama

Languages: Korean, Japanese, English


You downloaded a suspicious app and ended up locked in a studio apartment that previously belonged to Rika, the founder of the R.F.A. You take on her role in creating a successful fundraising party while unveiling the mystery behind her death and growing your relationship with the R.F.A members.

Taishou x Alice

★★★★★

Genre: Fairy Tale, Episodic

Languages: Japanese, English


You wake up with your memories gone. You stumble upon a mirror that transports you to the 'Looking-Glass World,' which is inhabited by gender-swapped fairytale characters. You become the heroine in their tales, and decide the end of their stories.

Hatoful Boyfriend

★★★★☆

Genre: Satirical Comedy

Languages: Japanese, English


You are the only human student at St. PigeoNation's Institute. After getting over your initial nervousness, you begin to... fall in love!?

Club Suicide

★★★★★

Genre: Drama

Languages: Japanese


After coming back to school, you spot a poster for an odd club: 'Club Suicide.' You decided to attend the club, and found five men who were serious about ending their lives in the seven days before graduation. Unable to tell them you didn't actually want to die, you became a witness to these seven days.
